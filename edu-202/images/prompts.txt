================================
Style for ALL images:
  Flat illustration cartoon, highly detailed.
  Background color #eef4ff (pale sky-blue).
  No text, no words, no letters, no labels, no UI strings, no captions anywhere in the image.
  Palette: deep navy and sky-blue, Zscaler blue theme with relevant accent colors.
  Dimensions: 1200 × 400 px (3:1 landscape banner).
  File format: PNG.
